More about the course

To meet the ever-growing market demand for software developers, a joint venture between VDAB, Obelisk and Multimedi brought us all together in this course.
Using C# as a base, this course is a full-fledged program covering every subject needed to start as an IT professional.
So far, the course covered subjects including C#, HTML 5, CSS3, XAML, JavaScript and Bootstrap. SQL, Scrum, Agile, Typescript and Angular8+ are still on the menu.
ASP.Net and .NET are reviewed in-depth throughout. Our focus is being able to contribute to a professional environment in a meaningful matter the moment we walk through the front door. This course is often compared to a computer science degree for aspiring high-level programmer without the parts that are irrelevant to our future employers.

More about the jobday

Software developers have been among the so-called bottleneck professions for decades. Data provided by VDAB even suggests it is in the top-3 most sought after profiles in Flanders at this time. This shortage of skilled and motivated software developers is only increasing because of the rapid expansion of the digital world. This job day aims to provide a solid answer to this. Come July 2021, we will be able to call ourselves the next generation .NET developers in C #. Because every single one of is eager to get started and get down to business, we want to explore the opportunities together with you. Urgently looking for new talent or simply curious to meet us?
